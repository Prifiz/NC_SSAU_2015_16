	Players are dealt 7 cards. The top card of the deck is turned face up and with it the game begins (this card is the beginning of the game deck).     
	The game begins in a clockwise direction.
	On his turn, each player must put a card in the game deck, and the card must match the top card in the game deck on color or image. If the player does not have a suitable card, then he takes one card from the deck of the bank, and if the card is suitable, it could make a move if he wants to. If a player has taken from the deck of the bank the right card, but she did not move - he receives no fine. The next player to act in a clockwise direction.
